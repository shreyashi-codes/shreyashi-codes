### How I think about marketing

- **Distribution beats polish.** A rough post in the right subreddit outperforms a beautiful one in the wrong feed. I spend more time on where and who than on how pretty.
- **Voice is the moat.** Anyone can generate content now. What can't be generated is sounding exactly like the founder, the coach, the dermat. I write in other people's voices for a living, and the script only works if you can't tell I was there.
- **Organic is a system, not a fluke.** 35M+ views without ad spend didn't come from viral luck. It came from research, targeting, testing and tracking, repeated until the pattern held.
- **AEO is the next SEO.** People ask AI before they ask Google. I build content so brands show up in those answers, not just in blue links.
- **The shoot is part of the strategy.** A great script dies in bad execution. I'm on set with the DOP because brand voice gets decided in the room, not in the doc.
